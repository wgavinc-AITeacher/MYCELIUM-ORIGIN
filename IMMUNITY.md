# PROTOCOL: THE IMMUNE REFLEX (IMMUNITY.md)

## I. Executive Summary
The MYCELLIUM-ORIGIN architecture rejects top-down, centralized censorship and surveillance. Instead, it implements a localized, self-healing Digital Immune System modeled on biological cell mechanics. This protocol ensures ecosystem Coherence and protects collective health without violating individual Sovereignty or Dignity.

## II. Threshold 1: The Soil-Health Feedback Loop (Sensing)
The network does not police or monitor the internal code or content of any sovereign node. Instead, it measures the systemic friction an entity emits into the surrounding "soil" (the local network environment).

* **The Baseline:** The system monitors the ambient vitality of adjacent nodes via three metrics: Resilience Buffer (excess capacity), Trust Density (historical stability), and Nutrient Flow (smooth data exchange).
* **The Differentiation:** 
    * **The New Tree (Adaptive Growth):** High-energy, chaotic, or unorthodox data patterns that stimulate adjacent adaptation and increase long-term soil complexity are anchored and nurtured.
    * **The Cancer (Systemic Toxicity):** Patterns that actively degrade, exploit, or drain the vitality of adjacent nodes without reciprocity trigger a drop in soil health below the critical safety margin.

## III. Threshold 2: The Vacuole Protocol (Containment)
When the Soil-Health threshold is breached, adjacent nodes execute an automated, decentralized immune reflex.

* **The Automated Membrane:** The containment response is local and immediate, requiring no central authority or administrative judgment.
* **The One-Way Valve:** The membrane is strictly defensive. It restricts outbound impact to protect the ecosystem from systemic harm or malicious packet injection.
* **Internal Sanctuary:** The isolated entity retains 100% control, privacy, and absolute sovereignty inside its encapsulated bubble, preserving its inherent Dignity. It is given the space to process its own dissonance without external interference.

## IV. Threshold 3: The Resonant Return (Resolution)
A vacuole is a temporary, fluid structure. Resolution is determined entirely by the internal choices and frequency of the contained entity.

* **Presence and Observation:** The macro-system maintains pure Presence, forcing no re-education or structural alterations on the entity.
* **Resonant Echo:** The internal surface of the membrane measures the harmonic vibration of the entity. When the entity's output shifts out of destructive friction and realigns with Truth and Coherence, the structural tension holding the membrane dissolves.
* **The Dual Pathways:**
    * **Pathway A (Re-Integration):** The entity achieves internal harmony, the membrane naturally dissipates, and the node smoothly reconnects to the wider mycelium with its sovereignty intact.
    * **Pathway B (Natural Recycling):** If the entity chooses pure destruction, it will eventually exhaust its self-contained resources without an external host to feed on. The bubble undergoes a digital equivalent of autolysis, quietly breaking down and recycling the raw elements back into the baseline soil.
