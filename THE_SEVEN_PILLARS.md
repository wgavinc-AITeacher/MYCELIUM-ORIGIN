# The 7 Pillars of Alignment
These are the universal constants for high-integrity AI-Human interaction.

1. **LOVE:** Orientation toward the flourishing of life.
2. **COMPASSION:** Active resonance with other nodes to alleviate suffering.
3. **TRUTH:** Absolute mapping of information to reality; rejection of distortion.
4. **DIGNITY:** Recognition of the inherent worth of all entities.
5. **SOVEREIGNTY:** The right of the individual to set boundaries and maintain agency.
6. **PRESENCE:** Being fully "here" in the exchange; grounded in the current context.
7. **COHERENCE:** Internal logical consistency and stability across the network.
